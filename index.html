What Are Carbon Credits?

Carbon credits allow companies to offset their carbon emissions by investing in renewable energy projects like ours. Each credit equals one ton of CO2 offset. By purchasing Earthbond carbon credits, your company contributes to sustainable development in Africa while reducing your carbon footprint.
